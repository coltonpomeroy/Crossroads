Introducing our community

Dear Friends,

Greetings from the Board of The Crossroads Renewal Project. We're excited to connect with our community of friends, family, and supporters who share our vision.

Our Vision

We are creating a haven of beauty—one that welcomes people from both near and far to work, rest, create, and access meaningful resources.

         

Introducing Our New Name & Website

After thoughtful conversation and feedback from many of you, we're proud to share our new name: Crossroads Commons.

The word “Crossroads” is fundamental to the deep identity of this place, while “Commons” speaks to what it represents—a shared space for all.

We invite you to explore our vision, meet our board, and get in touch at:
www.crossroadscommons.com

         

Construction Is Beginning

We're thrilled to announce our partnership with Hilltop Clinic. Hosting an excellent pediatric clinic is central to our mission, and we're grateful to the Hilltop team for bringing their care to Crossroads. We anticipate opening Hilltop at Crossroads in the first quarter of 2027.

We look forward to sharing more announcements as tenants finalize their plans. If you're interested in bringing your business or nonprofit to Crossroads Commons, we'd love to hear from you at info@crossroadscommons.com.
