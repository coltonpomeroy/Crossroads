Front Page Mockups

Five design directions for the Crossroads Commons website

General Concepts

Two foundational design directions exploring layout, tone, and visual hierarchy.

Concept A

Community Hub

Editorial magazine feel with large typography hero, alternating feature bands, split-layout CTA, and centered stack footer.

Concept B

Vibrant & Bold

Energetic split-screen hero with photo mosaic, stats bar, bold colored tiles, and gradient CTA banner.

Latin & Southwestern Concepts

Three culturally resonant designs inspired by Southwestern architecture, light and shadow, and botanical gardens.

Concept C New

Adobe Horizon

Southwestern warmth with desert mesa hero layers, arch-shaped feature icons, sunset gradient CTA, and stepped pyramid dividers.

Concept D New

Sol y Sombra

Bold light-and-shadow drama with dark hero, diagonal lattice patterns, 2+4 feature hierarchy, and clean white CTA.

Concept E New

Jardin

Organic botanical garden aesthetic with overlapping bloom circles, staggered masonry cards, garden-path dot dividers, and soft curves.

All mockups use brand colors, typography (Plus Jakarta Sans), and logos from the official Brand Style Guide v1.0. Concepts C, D & E draw from Latin and Southwestern design traditions.
